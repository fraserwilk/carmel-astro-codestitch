For Therapists

EMDR Supervision
& Case Consultation

Carmel Pound is one of only approximately 130 EMDRAA Accredited Consultants in Australia. She offers individual and group EMDR supervision and case consultation to therapists who want to deepen their EMDR practice and work towards professional accreditation.

Who this is for

Is this the right fit
for your stage of practice?

Carmel's consultation is best suited to therapists who have completed basic/level 1 training, advanced/level 2 training and/or are working their way to EMDR Accredited Practitioner status. If you are looking to build confidence, competence, and/or formal recognition in your EMDR practice, get in touch.

This service is for you if you are…
  • A trained therapist working toward EMDRAA accreditation (Level I or II)
  • An EMDRAA accredited practitioner seeking ongoing consultation hours
  • A therapist wanting to deepen your EMDR skills with complex presentations
  • A therapist based anywhere in Australia (telehealth available)

About Carmel's credentials

EMDRAA Accredited Consultant

Carmel holds the highest level of EMDR accreditation available through the EMDR Association of Australia — Accredited Consultant status. This designation recognises advanced clinical expertise in EMDR and the ability to supervise and co-facilitate training of other practitioners.

She is also an AHPRA-registered psychologist and Psychology Board of Australia approved supervisor, with over a decade of private practice experience in the Southern Highlands.

New: Carmel has recently been selected to join a small panel of EMDR Consultants providing EMDR case consultations to members of the Australian Psychological Society (APS).

About accreditation

Understanding EMDRAA accreditation

The EMDR Association of Australia (EMDRAA) offers a recognised accreditation pathway for therapists who have completed EMDR training.

Level I — EMDRAA Practitioner

Requires completion of EMDRAA-approved EMDR training plus a specified number of consultation hours with an Accredited Consultant. Consult the EMDRAA website for current requirements.

Consultation hour requirements

EMDRAA accreditation requires a set number of consultation hours with an Accredited Consultant. Individual and group consultation hours are counted differently — check EMDRAA requirements for specifics.

Telehealth consultation counts

Consultation hours undertaken via telehealth are recognised by EMDRAA for accreditation purposes, making Carmel's services accessible to therapists anywhere in Australia.

Please note: EMDRAA accreditation requirements can change. Carmel recommends checking the EMDRAA website directly for the most current requirements before commencing consultation.
